Multiplicity

Using a system as subject matter and variables like ways of communicating, mediums, and interactive experiences, I’ve combined elements in multiple ways. Or, I’ve pushed the same subject matter through different forms to achieve multiple readings.

Friction

Defining friction as an area of discomfort or limitation in personal agency within a system, I with points of resistance to make sense of their sources. I hope to open up possibilities of addressing, fixing, or overcoming these frictions.

Interaction

The places we inhabit are socially constructed and hold lots of histories. Design can create space for engagement among people to explore our varied and shared contexts and create moments of connection.

How can a multiplicity of voices and forms create space for nuance and multiple truths?

How can recontexutalizing experiences serve to shift power in order for us to see new possibilities of interacting and being?

How can moments of interaction and engagement connect people to each other and to the systems that they interface with every day?
